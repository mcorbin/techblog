A tour of Riemann : Installing

How to install Riemann ?

Installing Riemann

Go to http://riemann.io/ and download Riemann for your platform. You will also need a JDK (OpenJDK 8 for example).

Configuring Riemann JVM

On Debian, takes a look at /etc/default/riemann. On RHEL, it should be on /etc/sysconfig/riemann.

For this tutorial, you don’t need to modify this file.

Riemann is just a jar, so that’s it, you have installed Riemann :)
